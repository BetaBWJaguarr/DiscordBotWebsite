Moderation Discord Bot

The Moderation Discord Bot is a tool designed to keep your server organized, safe, and enjoyable. Our bot automatically detects malicious activities, manages users, and enhances your community's security.

Anti-Swear System

Detect and automatically warn users for inappropriate language. The advanced anti-swear system keeps your server clean from unwanted words.

Anti-Spam System

Prevent spam messages from flooding your server with our efficient anti-spam system. Keep the conversations meaningful and spam-free.

Warn-Kick-Ban Commands

Automate moderation actions like muting, warning, kicking, and banning users. Our bot helps you maintain order and discipline in your server.

Logging System

Track activities within your server and maintain logs for moderation actions. Never miss important events with our comprehensive logging features.

Voice Channel Swear Detection

Record and monitor swearing in voice channels to keep your server's verbal interactions safe and respectful.

Verification System

Manage and verify new members joining your server, ensuring a trusted and secure community environment.
